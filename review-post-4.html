"Stranger Things"

Episode Title: The Vanishing of Will Byers

Stars: ★★★★☆

Grade: B+

Nostalgia and Nightmares in Hawkins

The first episode of "Stranger Things" is a masterclass in 80s nostalgia and supernatural horror. We are introduced to a group of kids who love Dungeons & Dragons, and the mysterious disappearance of their friend, Will. The episode is dripping with atmosphere, from the synth-heavy soundtrack to the lovingly crafted period details. The performances are excellent, especially from the young cast, and the mystery is instantly compelling.

"Mornings are for coffee and contemplation."

Final Thoughts

A strong start to a global phenomenon. It's a show that wears its influences on its sleeve, but it's more than just a pastiche. It's a thrilling, scary, and surprisingly heartfelt adventure that will leave you wanting more.
