"Black Mirror"

Episode Title: The National Anthem

Stars: ★★★☆☆

Grade: C

A Shocking Start to a Shocking Series

The first episode of "Black Mirror" is a brutal, uncompromising, and deeply uncomfortable watch. It's a story about a prime minister who is forced to perform a depraved act on live television to save a kidnapped princess. The episode is a scathing satire of our media-obsessed culture, and it's a powerful statement of intent for the series. However, it's also a difficult and unpleasant watch, and it's not for the faint of heart.

"We're all watching. We're all part of it."

Final Thoughts

A bold and unforgettable start to the series, but it's one that will likely divide audiences. It's a powerful piece of television, but it's not one that I'll be revisiting anytime soon.
