Our Terrifying, Tech-Obsessed Future

"Black Mirror" | ★★★★★ | A+

"The National Anthem," the first episode of "Black Mirror," is a shocking and unforgettable introduction to the series. It's a dark, twisted, and thought-provoking exploration of technology, media, and the dark side of human nature. The episode is not for the faint of heart, but it's a powerful and important piece of television that will stay with you long after you've seen it.

"The future is already here – it's just not evenly distributed."

Final Thoughts

A bold and brilliant start to a groundbreaking series. "Black Mirror" is a must-watch for anyone who wants to understand the world we live in, and the world we may be heading towards.
