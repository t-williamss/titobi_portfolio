"Fleabag"

Episode Title: Season 2, Episode 1

Stars: ★★★★★

Grade: A

The Priest, the Fox, and the Fourth Wall

"Fleabag" returns with a season opener that is somehow even more brilliant than the first. The episode finds our titular anti-heroine navigating a tense family dinner, and introduces the now-famous "hot priest." Phoebe Waller-Bridge's writing is sharper than ever, and her performance is a masterclass in comedic timing and heartbreaking vulnerability. The fourth-wall breaks are used to devastating effect, drawing the viewer deeper into Fleabag's chaotic inner world.

"This is a love story."

Final Thoughts

A flawless start to a flawless season of television. It's a poignant, hilarious, and deeply human exploration of grief, faith, and the messy business of being alive. If you haven't seen "Fleabag," you are missing out on one of the best shows of the century.
