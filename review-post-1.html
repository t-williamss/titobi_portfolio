"The Midnight Gospel"

Episode Title: Taste of the King

Stars: ★★★★★

Grade: A+

A Cosmic Trip Worth Taking

The first episode of "The Midnight Gospel" is a whirlwind of psychedelic visuals and profound, often hilarious, philosophical discussion. The show follows Clancy, a spacecaster with a malfunctioning multiverse simulator, as he interviews beings from dying worlds. This episode features an interview with a zombie-fighting president who discusses the nature of consciousness and the pitfalls of drug policy. The animation is a chaotic marvel, perfectly complementing the free-flowing conversation.

"It's like a beautiful, cosmic dance... with zombies."

Final Thoughts

An absolute must-watch for anyone interested in animation, philosophy, or just a truly unique television experience. It sets a high bar for the rest of the series, promising a journey that is as thought-provoking as it is visually stunning.
